How to Play Pickleball

This is a scratch page created for IS 201 assignment requirements.

Jump down to the video

Follow these basic steps to start playing pickleball:

  1. Gather your equipment:
    • Pickleball paddle
    • Pickleball (plastic ball with holes)
    • Net and court with proper lines
  2. Choose partners and decide if you will play singles or doubles.
  3. Serve the ball underhand from behind the baseline, diagonally to the opposite service box.
  4. Let the ball bounce once on each side before volleying near the net.
  5. Play rallies until one side commits a fault or hits the ball out of bounds.
